About Pizza Yard

We are a family run Pizza Van, Bar and sit-in cafe in the southside of Glasgow.

Founded in 2020 by Sophie Hollis and Jillianne Rennie, here at Pizza Yard we are immensely proud of our pizzas, made lovingly from all locally sourced Scottish ingredients and produce.

Our pizzas are available for eat-in, collection or delivery.

Come and have a wee taste of Pizza Yard today.
